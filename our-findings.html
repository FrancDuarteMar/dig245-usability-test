Our Findings

1. Search for any term using the integrated search bar

Our test subject was able to quickly search for any term and select one of the available posts to browse. There was a slight interruption in his search by a pop up giving the user the option to sign up for the newsletter.

2. Join the mailing list

Our test subject was easily able to join the mailing list because as soon as he clicked on an article, he was prompted to join the mailing list. This was not easily replicable on other devices because the mailing list would not pop up.

3. Use the accessibility features to navigate to another article

Our test subject was not able to quickly use the accessibility features. The test subject gave up on navigating the page using only the keyboard until he was helped by a proctor.

4. Use the sections navigation menu to navigate to an article

Our test subject previously used this menu when they were looking for the newsletter sign up so it was an easy task for them. They completed this task without any obstacles.
